About us

We're a Minecraft Bedrock Edition server that focuses on fair play. We founded Versai because we noticed a disturbing trend in servers on the server listing website, where almost all top servers were created soley for profit alone. Our server is not pay to win, and purchases are cosmetic only.

Join our server

You can join our server at the IP versai.pro, default port, or just click here to add us. You can also check our server listing. We have lots of plans, and have gotten a lot of support recently, so expect more servers soon!

Content Creator?

We offer Famous and Famous+ ranks to content creators, the requirements for youtubers are 500 subscribers for Famous, and 2000 for Famous+. Simply tag us in our Discord with a link to a video on our server and we'll hook you up! Not a youtuber, no problem! Contact an owner on discord, and we'll see what we can do!
